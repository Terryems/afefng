About us

  What we do?

As a climate change NGO, our primary focus is to raise awareness about environmental issues, advocate for sustainable policies, and collaborate

with communities to implement eco-friendly practices. We engage in research, education, and outreach programs to address climate change impacts, promote renewable energy solutions, and encourage conservation efforts. Our goal is to inspire positive change, mobilize public support, and work towards a more sustainable and resilient future for the planet.

 Who we are

We are a dedicated team of individuals within the climate change NGO, united by a shared commitment to addressing environmental challenges.

Our diverse expertise ranges from environmental science to advocacy, allowing us to approach climate issues comprehensively. Together, we strive to make a meaningful impact by fostering awareness, driving positive policy changes, and empowering communities to take sustainable actions.

 Our Goals

Raise Awareness: Increase public understanding of climate issues through educational initiatives and outreach programs.

Advocate Policies: Work towards the implementation of sustainable policies at local, national, and global levels.
Promote Renewable energy: Support the transition to clean energy sources, advocating for renewable technologies and practices.
Community Engagement: Collaborate with communities to develop and implement eco-friendly solutions tailored to their unique needs.
Conservation Efforts: Contribute to biodiversity preservation, sustainable resource management, and the protection of ecosystems
Reasearch and Innovation: Conduct research to identify emerging challenges and promote innovative solutions for a more resilient future. Our diverse expertise ranges from environmental science to advocacy, allowing us to approach climate issues comprehensively. Together, we strive to make a meaningful impact by fostering awareness, driving positive policy changes, and empowering communities to take sustainable actions.

Our Team

Meet our Young, Vibrant and resourceful team..

Joy Ibejekwe

Gender Equality Coordinator
Gender Equality Coordinator

Ibejekwe Ogechukwu Joy is a dedicated meteorologist passionate about addressing climate change. She focuses on educating children and youth about sustainability and climate issues, using workshops and outreach programs in schools to promote climate literacy and practical environmental actions. Joy also works with communities to encourage sustainable agriculture, waste management, and water conservation, believing that empowering women and youth is key to building climate resilience. As Gender Equality Coordinator at AFEF, she ensures women’s voices are included in climate decision-making and mentors girls interested in science and environmental leadership. Joy advocates for integrating indigenous knowledge into climate adaptation strategies and collaborates with community leaders to promote traditional sustainable practices. Her research interests include climate variability, gender and climate resilience, and the role of education in driving change. Joy’s vision is to see a generation of environmentally conscious citizens equipped to tackle climate challenges. Through her dedication and leadership, she is making a lasting impact and inspiring others to join the fight for a sustainable future.
